Trail Etiquette

The following is a list of some trail etiquette you should keep in mind when hiking

  • Horses, hikers, and bikers all can use the trail. Horses should have priority, followed by hikers, then bikers
  • Step to the side of the trail to allow uphill traffic to pass
  • Leave the trail how you found it. Do not go off the path, nor leave any garbage on the trail
  • If listening to music or taking pictures, be mindful of your surrounds
  • Be polite, hikers always like to say Hi
